published on December 31, 2011
A new year is upon us, and I figured this would be good time to take a look at the improvements in the Mojolicious framework in the last year. There has been a dazzling number of releases, 122 in fact, including the current release, 2.42. Some of the releases were mere bugfix releases, with nothing noteworthy to add, but I’ll try going through the major releases in the last year.
