iPad as a writer' s tool

published on January 01, 2011
I believe I have found my ideal writing tool. For the last couple of months, I have been drafting text using iA Writer on the iPad. While the softward keyboard is cumbersome to use, I find the focus the combination gives very inspiring. Nothing but me and the text, no IMs or emails to disturb me.
