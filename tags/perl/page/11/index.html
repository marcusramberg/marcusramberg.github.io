Fixing the POD synopsis in OSX

published on May 18, 2009
Having problems copying example code from POD and getting invalid characters in your Perl? Apparently this is a problem with nroff on OS X (According to my linux hacking peeps), but the solution is quite simple. Just stuff this in your .profile or whatever ;) alias perldoc='perldoc -t' And your pod is rendered in glorious pure-text, without using weird characters for ' and -.
